BIO | Mojo Le Fay

Charlotte, NC

Voodoo King Records, Genuine Debbie D Records

Well known in the Carolinas as DJ Moejo (now known as Mojo Le Fay), she has made a name for herself as one of the top female Vinyl Djs over the last 7 years at clubs throughout the Southeastern United States. She has played at shows with an array of influential artists including Dj Icey, Rick West, Baby Anne, Plump Djs, AK1200, John B and Huda Hudia among others at venues all across the south. She has been honing her skills at record production for years and is now about to release her first sanctioned remix on Genuine Debbie D Records
H20's (USA) "Candy Painted" (Jackal and Hyde vs Mojo Le Fay remix)
